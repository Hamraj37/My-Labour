# Privacy Policy for My Labour

Last updated: June 15, 2026

At My Labour, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage

### A. Labour Data
To provide our core service of tracking labour attendance and payments, the app collects names, phone numbers, and attendance records of the workers you add. This data is synchronized with **Google Firebase** to ensure your data is backed up and accessible across your devices.

### B. Company Information
Company details (Name, Address, Phone Numbers) that you provide for report branding are **stored locally** on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This information is account-specific and is not uploaded to our servers.

### C. Signatures and Stamps
Signature images or stamps selected for reports are **stored locally** on your device. We do not transmit these images to any external servers.

### D. User Profile Information
Names and profile photos you customize within the app are **stored locally** on your device. While we use Google Authentication for login, any custom profile changes you make are kept on the device and isolated to your specific account.

## 2. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function correctly:
- **Camera**: Used for taking photos of workers or documents if required by the user.
- **Internet**: Required to sync labour data with Firebase and check for app updates via GitHub.
- **Notifications**: Used to send daily reminders to mark attendance and notify you of application updates.
- **Storage/Media**: Required to select signatures, logos, or profile photos from your gallery and to save generated PDF reports.

## 3. Data Security
We use Firebase's secure infrastructure to protect the data synchronized to the cloud. Local data is isolated per user account on the device to prevent unauthorized access between different users on the same device. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 4. Third-Party Services
Our app uses the following third-party services:
- **Google Firebase**: For authentication and database hosting.
- **GitHub**: For hosting the application source code and distributing updates.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through the GitHub repository.
